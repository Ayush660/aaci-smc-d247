Association of Alliance Clubs International (AACI)

Association of Alliance Clubs International (AACI) is a Public Company Registered with the objective of Social welfare activities under Section 8 of the Company’s Act 2013 , Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, India having its Registered Office In Delhi with Corporate Identity Number (CIN): U85190DL2017NPL315020

AACI is formed for charitable objects, the promotion of social welfare activities, art, science, sports, education, charity, protection of environment or any such other objective. It is a Non-Government Organisation (NGO) registered with Company Act 2013. The AACI is committed to use its donations , or voluntary contribution in promoting its objectives. The Association of Alliance Clubs International, Constitution & Bye-Laws and Policies are always framed keeping its objectives in mind.
Head Quarters and Administrative Office of the AACI is at : A-383, Sarita Vihar, New Delhi-110076, India.

About

Restless Worker & Tireless Creator

SLF LMF Ally Dr.M.Kallazhagar (Charter District Governor-247)

The best personal coach of Tamil Nadu, Excellent Puplic speaker, Provides skills development training for individuals, professional and educational institutions, school and college students. Director of Chisel Academy and Chisel Creations.Homoeopath and Psychologist.Has been running a private clinic in Kannamangalam since 1995.
He is a Multi talented Personality as Stage Drama Actor from 7 years old.Director,Lyric Writter and Public Speaker from 14 years old. Voice advertising,Eye Catching PPT,Motivation Song creator and Anchor.He has translated many books on leadership and training into Tamil. Familiar with photography and videography.
He was born in 25-09-1969 at Perumangkuppam located in Vellore District with one Yelder Sister and two elder brothers.His Father Mr.Munirasan as Retd. Headmaster and Mother Mrs.Radhabai as Home maker.He still lives with his parents and brother as a joint family.
His wife is LMF Ally Meena Kallazhagar,studied M.A.,B.Ed.,in Economics. His son is LMF Ally Er.K.Iyalezhilan,studied M.Tech., Software Engineering in VIT,and worked in Ajeya Infosol Ltd,Chennai as Digital Marketing SEO.His daughter is LMF Ally Dr.K.Kuralezhil,studying B.H.M.S., in Martin Homoeopathy Medical College at Coimabtore.
Served in the Lions Clubs International for 18 years as Charter Secretary, Treasurer, Secretary, President in Club Level and District Bulletin Editor, District Directory Editor- 2 Times, District Cabinet Secretary-2 Times, District Guest,District IT,District Liaison, District Ambassador, District Technologist,Coordinating Lion &GAV in District level positions.
He has amassed a number of awards of Par Excellence District Award-15, Par Excellence Multiple Award-9, International President Certificate-15, International President Leadership Medal-3,International President Presidential Medal-2, International Club Excellence President Award, International President 5 year Service Leo Advisor Award and has rendered invaluable service.
On 25th April 2021, he created 247 district in Association of Alliance Clubs International and rose to become its Charter District Governor and gave 30 Clubs & 110 LMF in 6 months, setting a world record for the first time in the history of the Association of Alliance Clubs International and winning the International Leadership Medal,14 star super leader Award and the Highest award of “Ambassador of Goodwill Award”.
