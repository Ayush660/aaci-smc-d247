District Governor
DG

SMF LMF Ally Dr.M.Kallazhagar

Charter District Governor-247

The best personal coach of Tamil Nadu, Excellent Puplic speaker, Provides skills development training for individuals, professional and educational institutions, school and college students. Director of Chisel Academy and Chisel Creations. He served in the Lions Clubs International for 18 years in more than twelve positions.
He was born in 25-09-1969 at Perumangkuppam located in Vellore District with one Yelder Sister and two elder brothers.His Father Mr.Munirasan as Retd. Headmaster and Mother Mrs.Radhabai as Home maker. He still lives with his parents and brother as a joint family.
He has amassed a number of awards of Par Excellence District Award-15, Par Excellence Multiple Award-9, International President Certificate-15, International President Leadership Medal-3,International President Presidential Medal-2, International Club Excellence President Award, International President 5 year Service Leo Advisor Award and has rendered invaluable service.On 25th April 2021, he created 247 district in Association of Alliance Clubs International and rose to become its Charter District Governor and gave 30 Clubs & 110 LMF in 6 months, setting a world record for the first time in the history of the Association of Alliance Clubs International and winning the International Leadership Medal,14 star super leader Award and the Highest award of “Ambassador of Goodwill Award”.
